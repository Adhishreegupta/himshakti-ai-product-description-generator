# Prompt Variation 1

## Prompt

Generate a product description for the following food product.

Product Name: {name}
Ingredients: {ingredients}
Weight: {weight}
Features: {features}
Tone: {tone}

## Example Input

- Product Name: Apple Jam
- Ingredients: Apple, Sugar
- Weight: 500g
- Features: No preservatives, Rich taste
- Tone: Premium

## Example Output

Introducing our Premium Apple Jam, crafted from fresh apples and high-quality ingredients. This delicious spread offers a rich fruity taste with no added preservatives, making it perfect for breakfast, desserts, and snacks. Enjoy authentic flavor in every spoonful.

# Prompt Variation 3

## Prompt

You are an expert food marketing specialist.

Generate a professional product description for an online marketplace.

Requirements:
- Around 100–150 words
- Highlight ingredients
- Mention weight
- Mention product benefits
- Match the selected tone
- End with a strong marketing sentence.

Product Details:
Name: {name}
Ingredients: {ingredients}
Weight: {weight}
Features: {features}
Tone: {tone}

## Example Input

- Product Name: Apple Jam
- Ingredients: Apple, Sugar
- Weight: 500g
- Features: No preservatives, Rich taste
- Tone: Premium

## Example Output

Enjoy the authentic taste of Premium Apple Jam made from fresh apples and carefully selected ingredients. Its rich texture and delicious flavor make it a perfect addition to breakfast, desserts, and snacks. Free from preservatives and packed in a convenient 500g jar, it offers both quality and freshness. Choose Premium Apple Jam for a delightful taste experience every day.

# Best Prompt

Prompt Variation 3 produced the best results. It generated well-structured, detailed, and professional product descriptions that were suitable for e-commerce platforms. It consistently included all important product details such as ingredients, weight, features, and tone while maintaining good readability. The output required minimal editing before use.
